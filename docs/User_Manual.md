## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application
Open your web browser and navigate to the application URL provided by your IT department. The application works on Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. It is also mobile-responsive.

### Logging In
1. Enter your registered **Email Address** and **Password**.
2. Click **Login**.
3. You will be redirected to your role-specific dashboard automatically.

### Changing Your Password
1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Change Password**.
3. Enter your current password, then your new password twice.
4. Click **Save**. You will be logged out and must log in again with the new password.

## Role: Chairman

As Chairman, you have full access to all modules.

### Dashboard
The Chairman Dashboard shows a real-time school-wide overview:
- **Department Health Cards** — Green (≥75% completion), Yellow (40–74%), Red (<40%).
- **KPI counters** — Total, Pending, In Progress, Completed, and Delayed tasks school-wide.
- **Charts** — Task status pie chart and department efficiency bar chart.

### Assigning Tasks
1. Go to **Task Assignment** in the sidebar.
2. Click **+ New Task**.
3. Fill in: Title, Description, Assign To (select a department head), Priority (High / Medium / Low), Start Date, Due Date.
4. Optionally attach a file (PDF, image, document — max 10 MB).
5. Click **Assign Task**.

The assigned user will receive an in-app notification and an email alert immediately.

### Monitoring Tasks
Go to **Task Monitoring** to see all tasks across all departments.
- Filter by Status, Priority, Department, or Date Range.
- Click any task row to view its full history log.
- Use the search bar to find specific tasks by title or keyword.

### User Management
1. Go to **User Management**.
2. To add a user, click **+ Add User**, fill in Name, Email, Password, Role, and Department.
3. To edit, click the **Edit** icon on any user row.
4. To deactivate an account, click the **Deactivate** button. The account is disabled but data is preserved.

### Approvals
Go to **Approval Management** to view and process Budget, Purchase, Policy, and Event approval requests submitted by department heads.
- Click **Approve** or **Reject**, add optional remarks, and confirm.

### Alerts & Escalations
The **Alerts & Escalations** page shows:
- Tasks past their due date (Delayed).
- Tasks with no update for more than 2 days.
- Escalated tasks that have been flagged to higher authority.

### Performance Analytics
View department efficiency scores, delay percentages, and monthly comparison charts.

### MIS Reports
1. Go to **MIS Reports**.
2. Select report type: Daily, Weekly, or Monthly.
3. Choose a date range.
4. Click **Generate Report**.
5. Download as **PDF** or **Excel**.

### Announcements
Go to **Announcements** to broadcast a message:
- **All Staff** — visible to every user on login.
- **Department** — visible only to the selected department.

## Role: Director

### Dashboard
Displays your assigned tasks and a school-wide department summary table.

### Task Updates
1. Go to **Task Updates**.
2. Your assigned tasks are shown as cards.
3. Click **Update Status** on any task.
4. Select the new status, add a comment, and optionally attach a file.
5. Click **Save Update**.

### Notifications
The bell icon (🔔) in the top navbar shows unread notification count. Click it to view all notifications.

## Role: Department Head (All Departments)

All department heads — Property, Finance, Admin, Principal, Admission, HR, Purchase, IT, Transport — share the same interface.

### Dashboard
- View all tasks assigned to you with their current status.
- KPI counters: Total, Pending, In Progress, Completed, Delayed.
- Task status pie chart.
- Recent announcements from the Chairman.

### Assigned Tasks
A detailed table of all your tasks with filter and search functionality.
- Filter by Status or Priority.
- Click a task title to view its full history.

### Updating a Task
1. On the Dashboard or Assigned Tasks page, find the task.
2. Click **Update Status**.
3. Choose the new status: **In Progress** or **Completed**.
4. Add a comment describing your progress.
5. Attach any relevant documents if required.
6. Click **Save**.

### Announcements
View all broadcast messages from the Chairman that are relevant to you or your department.

### Notifications
- In-app: Bell icon shows real-time alerts for new task assignments, deadline reminders, and escalation notices.
- Email: You will also receive email notifications for critical alerts.

## Task Status Workflow

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Task has been assigned but work has not yet started. |
| **In Progress** | You have started working on the task. |
| **Completed** | Task is done and marked as complete. |
| **Delayed** | Task has passed its due date without completion (set automatically). |
| **Escalated** | Task has been flagged by the system and escalated to higher authority. |

---

## Tips & Best Practices

- **Update tasks regularly** — even a short comment keeps stakeholders informed and prevents escalation.
- **Check the notification bell daily** — new assignments and reminders appear there.
- **Attach evidence** — upload completion documents, photos, or reports when marking tasks as completed.
- **Report issues early** — if you cannot complete a task by its deadline, contact the Chairman via the comments section before the due date.
